Fee Management

This feature facilitates streamlined and efficient management of student fee payments. It not only simplifies the process of fee tracking but also enhances communication between the coaching class, students, and parents while maintaining accurate financial records.

Accountability

Official receipts serve as proof of payment, reducing disputes or confusion.

Transparency

Students, parents, and administrators can easily monitor payment progress.

Archival Benefits

Receipts can be stored digitally for easy access and reference.

Track Student Fee Installment

Comprehensive Records

The platform maintains records of fee payments, installment schedules, and due dates for each student.

Real-Time Updates

The system reflects payment statuses in real-time, offering administrators an up-to-date overview.

Clear Visualization

Fee installment tracking provides a visual representation of payment trends and patterns.

Auto SMS Reminders to Students & Parents

Automated Reminders

The platform generates and sends SMS reminders to students and parents before fee due dates.

Personalized Content

Reminders can include specific fee amounts, installment details, and due dates.

Timely Notifications

Reminders are sent in advance to ensure timely payments and reduce late fees.

Generate Fee Receipts

Accessible Documentation

Professional Presentation
